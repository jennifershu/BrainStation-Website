Who We Are

Project E-Waste is a local organization that helps raise awareness to disposing electronic waste properly. We show people how throwing their electronics away is terrible for the environment. Our community needs to start somewhere and that's where we come in. Project E-Waste will help this community grow to become more aware of the mistake they are making to the environment.

Why E-Waste is Bad

Electronic don't seem to be hazardous when you look at them because of how pretty they look on the outside. The inner part of these devices is the part that poses a threat to our environment. Electronic products are filled with heavy metals, semi-metals and other chemical compounds that can leak into soil and become dangerous. Things like lead, mercury, copper, barium, nickel and even arsenic are all present within a variety of electronic products. As they are being thrown away or placed in the landfills, the products break which can expose the inner workings and those dangerous chemicals and metals.

Many of these chemicals and metals are known causes of serious health conditions like cancer, diabetes, impaired cognitive function, damaged organs and more. This is dangerous to both humans and animals which causes death then leads to less of a population of both human and animals!
